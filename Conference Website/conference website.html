Event Details

Date: January 1-3, 2025

Location: Buea, Cameroon

Description: The conference will be about web development and will typically be a dynamic and engaging event, bringing together professionals, experts, and enthusiasts from the software industry.

Speakers

Jonathan Snook:

Known for his work on web standards and CSS, Snook has been influential in shaping modern web design practices. He has worked with major companies like Shopify and Yahoo.

Brad Frost:

A web designer and consultant, Frost is a strong advocate for responsive web design. He has authored books and popularized concepts like Atomic Design, which has become widely adopted in the industry.

Sarah Drasner:

A developer, writer, and award-winning speaker, Drasner is known for her expertise in front-end development and animation. She has worked with companies like Microsoft and Adobe and is an advocate for accessibility and inclusive design.

Chris Coyier:

Founder of CSS-Tricks and co-founder of CodePen, Coyier is a prominent figure in the web design community. He shares valuable insights on CSS, front-end development, and best practices through his blog, podcast, and speaking engagements.
